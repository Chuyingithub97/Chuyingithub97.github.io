COVID-19 Pathogeny Symptom Transmission Channels Treatment

COVID-19

The COVID-19 is an infectious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). On February 11, 2020, the World Health Organization named it Corona Virus Disease 2019.

On January 30, 2020, WHO announced that the COVID-19 epidemic would be listed as a public health emergency of international concern (PHEIC).

Pathogeny

Coronavirus Disease 2019 is an acute infectious disease.

The pathogenesis of the virus can be understood more here: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0896841120300469.

Symptom

The main manifestations are fever, fatigue, and cough.

A small number of patients with nasal congestion, runny nose, sore throat and diarrhea and other symptoms.

Severe patients usually suffer from respiratory distress one week after the onset of the disease. In severe cases, they rapidly progress to acute respiratory distress syndrome and organ failure.

Transmission Channels

The transmission routes of novel coronavirus pneumonia are direct transmission, aerosol transmission and contact transmission.

Direct transmission refers to the infection caused by the sneezing, coughing and speaking droplets of patients, and the exhaled gas is inhaled directly in a short distance; aerosol transmission refers to the mixture of droplets in the air, forming an aerosol, which causes infection after inhalation; contact transmission refers to the deposition of droplets on the surface of objects, after touching the contaminated hands, and then touching the mucous membranes of mouth, nose, eyes, etc., leading to infection.

Treatment

Detection technology of prevention and diagnosis of patients, high-sensitivity quantitative PCR kit technology, antibody drugs and vaccines, indoor and building air anti-virus technology, protein-based virus transmission blocker, stem cell inflammatory drugs, epidemiological research and psychological rehabilitation system.

All Over the World

As of May 2020, the virus has spread all over the world, with confirmed cases at more than 4,760,000 and confirmed deaths exceeding 318,000.

In China

The pandemic was first recorded in Wuhan, China. Shortly after detection, the virus broke out across the nation.

On the Economy

COVID-19 caused a global recession. Countless jobs were lost and confidence in markets decreased. .

On Transport and Mobility

Many countries announced social distancing policies that affected mobility and access worldwide.
